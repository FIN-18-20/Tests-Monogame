Controls
--------
Left/Right arrow keys to move left or right
A/D keys to move left or right
Up/Down arrow keys to Jump or Crouch
W/S keys to Jump or Crouch

B to shoot fireball while with Fireball Mario
R to reset to the main menu
Enter to pause the game and to make selection on the Main Menu
